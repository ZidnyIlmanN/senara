The Core Ingredient

The Power of Pineapple Enzymes

At the heart of SENARA lies Bromelain, a proteolytic enzyme extracted from the stem and fruit of pineapples. Known for its remarkable anti-inflammatory and exfoliating properties, we've refined this botanical wonder into a clinical-grade active.

THE JOURNEY

From Farm to Laboratory

A meticulous journey of quality control and scientific refinement, from sustainable soil to clinical-grade actives.

Step 01

Ethical Sourcing

Our journey begins in the volcanic soil of Costa Rica, where pineapples are grown using regenerative organic practices that preserve biodiversity and ensure the highest concentration of raw enzymes.

Step 02

Molecular Refining

Using cold-pressed extraction, we isolate Bromelain at its peak potency without thermal degradation.

Step 03

Clinical Validation

Every batch undergoes rigorous dermatological testing to ensure biocompatibility and visible efficacy.

OUR COMMITMENT

Leaving Only a Glow Behind.

Zero Waste Circularity

We utilize the entire pineapple fruit. Stems for enzymes, pulp for exfoliants, and skins for nutrient-rich compost returned to the farms.

Infinite Glass Packaging

95% of our packaging is infinitely recyclable glass. We avoid plastic components wherever possible to minimize our oceanic footprint.

THE VISIONARIES

Dr. Elena Thorne & Sarah James

"We started SENARA because we were tired of choosing between 'natural' products that didn't work and 'scientific' ones that felt clinical and soul-less."

Elena, a biochemist with 15 years in dermatological research, and Sarah, a botanical cultivator, merged their worlds to create a brand that honors the intelligence of nature with the rigor of modern science.
